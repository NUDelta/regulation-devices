Conceptual Problem

Definition

  • A translation of your practical problem in terms of sections or subsections of the Canvas that is more specific

Scaffolding Questions

  • What part of the Canvas does this blocker relate to?
  • Are you confident in what you’ve filled out so far?
  • What are the next empty sections of the Canvas?

Example

  • “We need to know what our interface and system arguments are because they inform what we end up building and what study measurements are measuring for. Specifically checking for alignment across our outcomes and characteristics”
