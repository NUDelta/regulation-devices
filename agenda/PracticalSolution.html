Practical Solution

Definition

  • How you’re going to get that information. (Sometimes this can just be the execution of the conceptual solution)

Scaffolding Questions

  • Do you have a structured process to get the information you want?
  • Can you break your conceptual solution into tasks? How?

Example

  • “Identify what features map to what outcomes, and then pick which outcomes we can reasonably test based on our current ideas and arguments we have for them”
