Questions and Concerns

When filling out this section of the agenda, please list any questions or concerns you might have with a specific phase for a topic. This can be to go over during SIG or just so your mentor is aware. 

Also if you feel weak about any of your above articulations, use this as a space to explain why briefly so you can discuss this with your mentor.
